What is Looker?

Looker, now part of Google Cloud, is reinventing business intelligence for the modern company. Looker works the way the web does, and its unique modeling language lets any employee leverage the work of your best data analysts. Operating 100% in-database, Looker capitalizes on the newest, fastest analytic databases to get real results, in real-time. Looker's lightweight open architecture makes it easy for developers to quickly and flexibly build, deploy, and iterate custom on data applications.

    Health, Wellness and Fitness

    "Looker is a solid BI tool"

    5.0

    May 31, 2025

    Overall, I've had a really good time with Looker. It's a solid BI platform that truly shines in how it handles data and lets people help themselves. LookML is a game-changer for keeping our data clean and empowering users. It's totally helped us understand our data better. Even though I sometimes wish for cooler native charts, Looker's flexibility and data modeling capabilities definitely make up for it. It's a fantastic choice if you need a powerful and adaptable BI tool.

    Pros

    Looker's flexibility is awesome, especially with its LookML modeling. It means we define our business logic once, and it's consistent everywhere. This makes it super easy for anyone to dig into the data without needing to know complex code. Plus, quickly creating new reports and drilling down is a breeze. Version control for LookML is also a huge win for teamwork!

    Cons

    My main gripe is with the visualizations. Looker's great for data, but I really wish it had more advanced and complex chart types built-in. Sometimes, for really specific visuals, I have to export data or use other tools, which isn't ideal. While you can build custom visuals, it's not as straightforward as I'd like, and the standard options feel a bit basic for telling richer data stories.
